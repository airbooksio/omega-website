Company

Built by operators, for operators.

Airbooks was founded by capital markets veterans who spent over a decade running desks, building tools, and watching the same broken infrastructure hold the market back.

Mission

Open infrastructure for fixed-income capital markets.

We're building the workflow software fixed-income teams have been asking for, and the open data and governance layer underneath that ends vendor lock-in for good. The point isn't to swap one walled garden for another — it's to give the market infrastructure it can actually trust.
