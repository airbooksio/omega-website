Products

Built for the work your team actually does.

Four products, one platform underneath. Each runs independently, integrates with what you already have, and gets better on OMEGA — but doesn't require it.

Sell-side

Postgame

Post-trade allocation, automated.

The most manual process in the primary market — final allocation, designations, audit trail — handled in a single collaborative environment. Replaces spreadsheets and phone calls with a clean, auditable record.

Key features

  • Real-time allocation collaboration across syndicate
  • Designation tracking with full audit trail
  • Compliance-grade record export
  • Direct integration with OMS / book-building systems

For: Sell-side syndicate desks, allocations, compliance.

Underwriters

Thunderwriter Pro

Competitive bidding management.

The first dedicated competitive bidding platform for municipals. Project bidding levels across multiple dealers, see real-time win-probability feedback, and skip the low-conviction bids.

Key features

  • Multi-dealer bid level projection
  • Real-time win-probability modeling
  • Historical bid analytics
  • Calendar integration with primary market data

For: Underwriters, competitive desks.

Buy-side

Buyside Suite

Primary calendar, order entry, and competitive management — built for the buy side.

Three integrated tools, designed to plug into existing OMS infrastructure.

Calendar Manager

A primary market viewer that gives portfolio managers a full picture of what's coming, what's relevant, and what to track.

Order Entry

The first market-wide electronic order entry system in municipals. Submit orders directly to syndicate without phone or fax.

Competitive Bid Manager

Surfaces upcoming competitive deals worth your team's time, with bid analytics and direct-to-desk routing.

For: Asset managers, buy-side PMs, traders.

Migration

Sync Bridge

Don't choose between old and new.

  • Configurable cutover paths per workflow
